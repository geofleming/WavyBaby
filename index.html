So.... We're Having a Baby!

Blog posted 28 Jun 2017

Well I say "we", its all Louise doing the work whilst I have the congratulatory slap on the back and pints in the pub!

Louise and the baby are doing well, now we're over the initial getting to know each other phase by draining Louise's energy on a daily basis, otherwise known as Trimester 1.

We then thought how can we get everyone involved and keep up-to-date on progress.

We have put together this website not only to show off my amazing new talents, thank you very much, but also too keep everyone involved. We got the idea of Wavy Baby from the 11 week scan in which the Sonographer capture the perfect image of the baby waving to us...or at least thats what we thought!

We have posted to the world wide web our precious baby mug shots, otherwise known as the scans, and whilst Louise allows it we'll post progress of the bump as we go.

I hope you all like the site and get some good feeling from it. We will build the site as we go along and will get you more involved in some hilarity from any ideas we come up with. Also open to any ideas you guys have too.

Love you all...from the 3 of us...Louise, George and Wavy Baby xxx.
